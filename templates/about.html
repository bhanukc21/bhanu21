Developed a Website Interface System to Effectively See and Visualize Trending Posts in Various Domains

What We Do

We have developed a website interface system that allows you to effectively visualize and analyze trending posts across various domains such as:

  • Politics
  • Movies
  • Sports
  • crime
  • education

Our system collects social media content from various platforms and employs advanced algorithms to identify and categorize trending posts in real-time.

Data Collection and Importing

We collect social media content from various platforms and import it into our system for analysis.

Preprocessing

The collected data undergoes preprocessing to clean, filter, and normalize it for further analysis.

Feature Extraction

We extract relevant features from the preprocessed data to capture key characteristics of the content.

How We Do It

Our system utilizes cutting-edge machine learning algorithms to analyze vast amounts of social media content in real-time. Key features include:

Dimensionality Reduction

Our system applies t-distributed Stochastic Neighbor Embedding (t-SNE) for dimensionality reduction to visualize high-dimensional data in a lower-dimensional space.

Clustering using DBSCAN

We use the Density-Based Spatial Clustering of Applications with Noise (DBSCAN) algorithm to cluster similar social media posts based on their features.

Prediction

Our system predicts trends and identifies emerging topics by analyzing clustered data and patterns.

Results Displaying

Users can explore and visualize the results through an interactive interface, including charts, graphs, and heatmaps.
